# v0.3-01: 5 New Slide Templates

**Type**: AFK
**Blocked by**: None — can start immediately

## What to build

Expand the template system from 3 templates to 8, adding 5 new slide templates. Each template must be a complete, renderable design skeleton that AI can select and parameterize.

New templates to add:

1. **poetic_landscape** (诗意风景) — for nature, landscapes, outdoor scenes. Full-screen photo with soft gradient overlay and poetic caption placement.
2. **magazine_left** (杂志式左图右文) — photo on the left (~60% width), caption/meta panel on the right. Suited for life photos and portraits.
3. **gallery_center** (艺术馆居中) — photo centered with generous padding, museum-style presentation. Suited for photography and artistic shots.
4. **dark_exhibition** (暗色展厅) — dark background, photo with subtle glow/border, dramatic presentation. Suited for night scenes and dramatic compositions.
5. **pet_portrait** (宠物肖像) — photo prominently centered with warm tones, circular or heavily rounded photo frame. Suited for pet close-ups.

Each template definition in `slide_templates.json` must include:
- Full `slots` (photo, caption, timeline regions with defaultBounds)
- Full `defaultParams` (photoFit, photoRadius, captionPlacement, timelineVariant, vignetteEnabled, captionPanelEnabled)
- Full `paramSchema` (allowed values/ranges for each param)
- `allowedLayerTypes` list
- `maxExtraLayers` count
- `preferredCategories` mapping

The backend fallback design generator must be able to use any of the new templates based on photo category and orientation.
